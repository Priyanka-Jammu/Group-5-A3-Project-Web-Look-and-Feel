Data Visualization

Below are interactive charts showing various statistics about our book collection and usage patterns. These visualizations help us understand our community better and make informed decisions about our service.

Books by Genre

This bar chart shows the distribution of books across different genres in our collection. Fiction and Non-Fiction are the most common genres, followed by Mystery and Science Fiction.

Borrowed vs Available Books

This pie chart illustrates the current status of books in our system. Approximately 65% of books are available for borrowing, while 35% are currently borrowed by users.

Monthly Book Sharing Trend

This line chart shows the trend of book sharing over the past year. There has been a steady increase in book sharing activity, with peaks during summer months and around the holiday season.
